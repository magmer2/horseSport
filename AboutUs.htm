About Us

Shopping at Six Horsemen is shopping for the real sports connoisseur.
This is because we exclusively stock top quality brands and our team is always on the lookout to make sure we have the latest and greatest in sports gear.

We stock brands like Nike, Adidas and Under Armour that are known for their high performance as well as their style. We sell gear for the fans such as All Blacks and NRL supporters Jerseys. We also sell athletes signiture gear such as items from Nike's Lebron James Collection.
